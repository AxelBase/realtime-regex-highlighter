Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: November 29, 2025

At Real-Time Regex Highlighter, your privacy is not just respected — it is guaranteed by design.

We Collect Nothing

This tool runs 100% in your browser. When you paste text, write patterns, or toggle flags:

  • No data is sent to any server — ours or anyone else’s
  • No logs are created
  • No IP addresses are recorded
  • No analytics, cookies, fingerprints, or tracking pixels are used

Your regex experiments — even sensitive log files, passwords, or internal data — never leave your device.

How It Works (And Why It’s Private)

All regex parsing, highlighting, URL compression, and sharing happen locally using JavaScript. The only external request is the initial page load (HTML, CSS, JS) from GitHub Pages or your own host. After that, it works fully offline.

Third-Party Services

We do not use:

  • Google Analytics, Plausible, Umami, or any analytics
  • Advertising networks
  • Social media widgets
  • External fonts that track users

The only CDN used (Bootstrap Icons) is privacy-respecting and does not set cookies.

Shared URLs (?s=…)

When you share a pattern via URL, your text and regex are compressed and stored only in the URL itself. Anyone with the link can view it — just like sharing a Google Doc link. We do not store, index, or log these URLs.

Data Security

Since no data is transmitted or stored, there is no database to breach. Your security depends only on your browser and device — exactly as it should be.

Children’s Privacy

This tool is safe and appropriate for users of all ages. No personal information is ever requested or collected.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy if new features are added. Changes will be announced on the tool and GitHub repository.

Contact

Questions? Open an issue on GitHub.

You own your data. Always have. Always will.
Thank you for trusting a truly private regex tool.
